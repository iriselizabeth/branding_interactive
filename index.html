About

A two day conference presented by the Department of Graphic Design, Animation and Game Art and Illustration at MECA&D.

Futurespective is a two-day conference celebrating the future of design, motion graphics, illustration, and digital media. Hosted at the Maine College of Art & Design, this event brings together industry-leading creatives, educators, and emerging talent for an immersive experience filled with inspiring keynotes, hands-on demos, thought-provoking panel discussions, and networking opportunities.

Attendees will explore cutting-edge digital tools, engage in ethical discussions about AI’s impact on design, and gain insights on how to start building a creative career. The event also features professional headshot sessions, portfolio critiques, and meet-and-greets with top studios.

The conference concludes with a keynote from Sarah Lucero from Chromatic Theory and an evening celebration on Congress Street with food trucks, live music, and a set by DJ VØID and DJ ASAP.

Whether you're a student, professional, or design enthusiast, Futurespective is your gateway to the evolving landscape of visual storytelling.
